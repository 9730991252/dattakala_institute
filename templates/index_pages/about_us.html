Dattakala Shikshan Sanstha is situated on a picturesque hilltop with a panoramic view of the Sahyadri Ranges. The campus offers a pollution-free, peaceful environment ideal for the holistic growth of students. With a focus on knowledge, professionalism, and value-based education, DKSS ensures the highest quality faculty committed to shaping student personalities.

From K.G. to P.G., Dattakala provides education in Pharmacy, Engineering, Management, and more, nurturing young minds through academics, sports, and co-curricular activities.

In today’s competitive and fast-paced world, we focus on building character, competence, and compassion — equipping students to become excellent professionals and even better human beings.

“We educate not just for careers, but for life.”
